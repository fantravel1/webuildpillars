How It Works

Your path to a premium domain, from application to ownership.

The Process

Five simple steps to claiming your digital identity.

1

Check Eligibility

Verify that you or your organization belongs to a pillar community (Black, Brown, Queer, Indigenous, neurodiverse, female, migrant, or differently abled) and fits one of our three focus areas.

2

Submit Application

Complete our domain request form with information about your organization, mission, current digital presence, and how a premium domain would support your work.

3

Review Process

Our team reviews applications within 5-7 business days. We evaluate mission alignment, community impact, and readiness to maximize the domain's potential.

4

Domain Matching

If approved, we present domain options from our portfolio that align with your mission and brand identity. You choose the one that fits best.

5

Transfer & Support

Once you select a domain, we transfer full ownership and provide ongoing support based on your commitment tier. The domain is yours—forever.

Who Qualifies?

We prioritize organizations and individuals from pillar communities—those who are Black, Brown, Queer, Indigenous, neurodiverse, female, migrant, or differently abled. Within these communities, we focus on three pillars:

Nonprofits & Social Enterprises

  • Registered 501(c)(3) or equivalent
  • Community-based organizations
  • Social enterprises with mission focus
  • Grassroots advocacy groups
  • Mutual aid organizations

Artists & Creators

  • Visual artists and designers
  • Musicians and performers
  • Writers and poets
  • Filmmakers and content creators
  • Collectives and creative cooperatives

Knowledge Builders

  • Educators and academic researchers
  • Cultural documentarians
  • Community archivists
  • Open-source contributors
  • Thought leaders and public intellectuals

Common Questions

How long does the process take?

From application to domain transfer typically takes 2-4 weeks. The review process is 5-7 business days, followed by domain matching and transfer coordination.

Is there really no cost?

The domain itself is completely free. You'll be responsible for ongoing costs like hosting, email services, and annual domain renewal (typically $10-20/year for standard TLDs).

Can I request a specific domain?

You can indicate preferences, but we match from our existing portfolio. We'll present options that align with your mission—you may discover an even better fit than you imagined.

What if I'm an individual, not an organization?

Individual artists, creators, and knowledge builders are absolutely eligible. Your personal brand and professional identity matter just as much as organizational ones.
