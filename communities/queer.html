Visibility Matters—Online and Off

The LGBTQ+ community has always understood the power of visibility. From the first pride marches to viral coming-out videos, being seen has been central to the movement. That visibility must extend to the digital realm.

Yet LGBTQ+ organizations—especially those serving the most marginalized within our community—often struggle with digital infrastructure. Trans-led nonprofits operate on shoestring budgets. Queer youth programs lack resources for professional websites. BIPOC LGBTQ+ groups face compounded barriers to funding.

Meanwhile, anti-LGBTQ+ organizations command premium domain names, creating an imbalanced digital landscape where hate appears more "established" than love.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can LGBTQ+ organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. LGBTQ+ nonprofits, queer artists, transgender creators, and pride organizations can apply for free premium domains. We gift domains valued from $500 to over $100,000 to qualifying organizations serving the queer community.

Do you prioritize trans-led organizations?

We recognize that trans-led organizations often face additional funding barriers. While we don't formally prioritize, we ensure trans-led groups receive equitable consideration and understand the unique challenges they face.

What about LGBTQ+ individuals, not just organizations?

Yes, individual queer artists, creators, educators, and activists can apply. You don't need to be a registered nonprofit—we support individual creators whose work benefits the LGBTQ+ community.

Is my privacy protected?

Absolutely. We understand the safety concerns many in our community face. We offer WHOIS privacy protection and will never publicly disclose your identity or organization details without explicit consent.

Ready to Claim Your Digital Pride?

If you're an LGBTQ+ creator, organization, or advocate making impact in your community, you may qualify for a premium domain gift worth thousands of dollars.
