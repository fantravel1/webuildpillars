Description of Services

We Build Pillars operates a domain gifting program for qualifying nonprofits, artists, and knowledge builders from pillar communities. Our services include:

  • Domain matching and transfer
  • Technical guidance and support (tiered)
  • Educational resources and content

Eligibility

To be eligible for our domain gifting program, applicants must:

  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Represent a nonprofit, social enterprise, artistic project, or knowledge-building initiative
  • Be part of or primarily serve pillar communities as defined on our website
  • Provide accurate and truthful information in applications

Application Process

Submitting an application does not guarantee receipt of a domain. We review all applications and make decisions based on alignment with our mission, available domains, and organizational capacity. Our decisions are final.
