The Challenge

How We Help

We gift premium .org, .net, and other TLD domains valued between $500 and $100,000+ to qualifying nonprofits. These are memorable, brandable names that establish instant credibility.

Who Qualifies?

We prioritize nonprofits and social enterprises led by or primarily serving pillar communities. This includes:

Registered Nonprofits

501(c)(3) organizations and international equivalents with demonstrated community impact.

Social Enterprises

Mission-driven businesses reinvesting profits into community benefit.

Grassroots Groups

Community organizations working toward formal nonprofit status.

Mutual Aid Networks

Collective care organizations providing direct community support.

Advocacy Organizations

Groups fighting for policy change and community justice.

Community Foundations

Local foundations serving specific geographic or identity-based communities.
