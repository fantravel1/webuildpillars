Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Our Approach to Cookies

We Build Pillars takes a minimal approach to cookies. We believe in respecting your privacy while providing a functional website experience. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

Cookie Purpose Duration
language_preference Remembers your language selection (EN/ES/FR) 1 year

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance the functionality of the website:

Cookie Purpose Duration
form_submitted Prevents duplicate form submissions Session

Third-Party Cookies

We minimize third-party cookies. The following services may set cookies:

  • Google Fonts: Used for typography. Google may collect anonymous usage data.
  • Formspree: Used for form processing. See their privacy policy.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through settings
  • Private Browsing: Use private/incognito mode to limit cookie storage
  • Third-Party Tools: Browser extensions can help manage cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our website.

How to Manage Cookies in Popular Browsers

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to remember your language preference. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us.
