Preguntas Frecuentes

Can social enterprises get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares supports social enterprises and mission-driven businesses, especially those led by founders from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What qualifies as a social enterprise?

Social enterprises are businesses where social or environmental impact is central to the mission—not just corporate social responsibility, but core to how the business operates and measures success.

Do I need to be a nonprofit?

No. Social enterprises can be for-profit, nonprofit, or hybrid structures. What matters is that social impact is central to your mission and you're led by or serving pillar communities.
