Supporting LGBTQ+ Digital Presence

LGBTQ+ organizations do essential work—from advocacy and healthcare to community building and crisis support. A professional digital presence helps these organizations reach people who need them, often in situations where visibility and trust are critical.

But queer organizations often face unique funding challenges, and premium domains can seem like an unaffordable luxury. Meanwhile, a professional domain like QueerComunidad.org establishes immediate credibility that queercommunity.wixsite.com simply cannot match.

We Build Pilares recognizes LGBTQ+ communities as a priority pillar community. We gift premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to organizations serving the queer community, helping build the digital infrastructure these vital organizations deserve.

Preguntas Frecuentes

Can LGBTQ+ nonprofits get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares includes LGBTQ+ and queer communities as priority pillar communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying LGBTQ+-led organizations and groups serving the queer community.

What types of LGBTQ+ organizations qualify?

We support LGBTQ+ advocacy organizations, pride committees, queer community centers, trans rights groups, LGBTQ+ health organizations, queer arts and cultural organizations, support groups, and any nonprofit primarily serving the LGBTQ+ community.

Why does We Build Pilares support LGBTQ+ organizations?

LGBTQ+ communities face unique challenges in establishing digital presence, from online harassment to funding limitations. A professional domain provides credibility and helps LGBTQ+ organizations reach those who need their services safely and effectively.

Do I need 501(c)(3) status?

No. We accept applications from registered nonprofits, grassroots organizations, community groups, and even individual queer artists and creators. The key is mission alignment and community service, not formal nonprofit status.
