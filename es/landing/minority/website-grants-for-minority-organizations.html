Website Grants for Minority Organizations

Premium domain grants that provide lasting value for underrepresented communities.

A Different Kind of Grant: Assets, Not Just Funding

Traditional website grants give you money that gets spent on development or hosting. Our approach is different: we give you a valuable asset that you own permanently.

Premium domain names are digital real estate. They're scarce, valuable, and appreciate over time. A domain grant from We Build Pilares provides a foundation worth $500 to $100,000+ that becomes more valuable as your organization grows.

This asset-based approach to supporting minority organizations creates lasting impact. You're not just getting help building a website—you're receiving digital wealth that stays with your community.

What Our Domain Grants Include

Premium Domain Asset

Brandable, memorable domains valued at $500–$100,000+. Not random URLs—real words and meaningful phrases that establish credibility.

Full Asset Transfer

Unlike grants with restrictions, the domain transfers to your organization completely. You own it, you control it, you keep it.

No Reporting Requirements

Traditional grants often require extensive reporting. Our domain grants are simple: we give, you receive, you build.

Soporte de Estrategia Digital

Higher tiers include guidance on maximizing your domain's value through effective website development and SEO.

Minority Organizations We Support

We define "minority organizations" broadly to include organizations led by or primarily serving underrepresented pillar communities:

BIPOC-Led

Black, Indigenous, and People of Color-led organizations and businesses.

LGBTQ+-Led

Queer-led organizations and those serving LGBTQ+ communities.

Women/Non-Binary-Led

Organizations led by women or non-binary individuals.

Immigrant-Led

Organizations led by immigrants or serving immigrant communities.

Disability-Led

Organizations led by differently abled individuals or serving disability communities.

Minority-Serving

Organizations whose primary mission is serving any underrepresented community.

Preguntas Frecuentes

What website grants are available for minority organizations?

We Build Pilares provides premium domain grants to minority-led organizations. Unlike traditional grants that provide cash, we gift valuable domain names worth $500 to $100,000+. This asset-based approach provides lasting value that appreciates over time.

How do domain grants differ from traditional website grants?

Traditional website grants provide funding that gets spent. A domain grant from We Build Pilares provides an asset you own permanently. The domain transfers to your organization, builds value through SEO, and serves as the foundation for all your digital activities.

Who qualifies as a minority organization?

We support organizations led by or primarily serving pillar communities: Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women, immigrants, refugees, differently abled, and neurodiverse communities. Both minority-led and minority-serving organizations qualify.

Is this a competitive grant process?

We review all applications and match qualifying organizations with appropriate domains. It's not a traditional competitive grant—we're looking for mission alignment and community impact, not polished proposals.
