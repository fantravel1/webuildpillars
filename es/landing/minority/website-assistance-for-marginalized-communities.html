Website Assistance for Marginalized Communities

Premium domain gifts for organizations doing essential community work.

Digital Tools for Comunidad Power

Marginalized communities do essential work with limited resources. While well-funded organizations have professional websites and memorable domains, community groups often make do with free subdomains and complicated URLs.

We Build Pilares changes this by gifting premium domain names—worth $500 to $100,000+—to organizations serving marginalized communities. A professional domain like YourOrganization.org establishes credibility that opens doors with funders, media, and government agencies.

Your organization does the hard work of community building. Let us provide the digital foundation you deserve.

Preguntas Frecuentes

What website assistance is available for marginalized communities?

We Build Pilares provides premium domain names as gifts to marginalized communities. These domains are valued at $500 to $100,000+ and give your organization a professional web address that establishes credibility with funders, media, and the public.

Who qualifies for website assistance?

We support organizations and individuals from marginalized communities including Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women-led, immigrant, disabled, and neurodiverse groups. Both registered nonprofits and community-based organizations qualify.

Is there really no cost for the domain?

Correct. Qualifying organizations receive premium domains as outright gifts. There's no purchase price, no revenue sharing, and no ongoing obligations. You own the domain completely.
