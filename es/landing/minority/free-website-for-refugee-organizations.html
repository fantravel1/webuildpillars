Free Website for Refugee Organizations

Refugee organizations can receive free premium domains worth $500-$100,000+.

Supporting Refugee Digital Presence

Refugee-serving organizations provide lifesaving support—from resettlement assistance and legal aid to cultural integration and community building. A professional digital presence helps these organizations connect with families who need their services.

Refugee organizations often work with communities facing language barriers and unfamiliarity with local systems. A professional, trustworthy domain name helps establish credibility and makes services easier to find and verify.

We Build Pilares supports organizations serving refugee communities, with special encouragement for refugee-led organizations. We gift premium domains to help build the digital infrastructure these vital community resources deserve.

Preguntas Frecuentes

Can refugee organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares supports organizations serving refugee and asylum-seeker communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying refugee-serving organizations and resettlement agencies.

What types of refugee organizations qualify?

We support resettlement agencies, refugee advocacy groups, asylum legal services, cultural integration programs, refugee-led community organizations, and any nonprofit primarily serving refugee and asylum-seeker communities.

Can refugee-led organizations apply?

Absolutely. We particularly encourage applications from refugee-led organizations where community members themselves are leading the work.
