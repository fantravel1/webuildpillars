Minority-led nonprofits face a technology gap. While larger organizations have IT departments and professional websites, community-based organizations often struggle with outdated tools and unprofessional web presence.

We Build Pilares addresses this inequity by gifting premium domain names—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to minority-led nonprofits. A professional domain like YourNonprofit.org immediately elevates how funders, media, and the public perceive your organization.

Preguntas Frecuentes

What technology support is available for minority nonprofits?

We Build Pilares provides premium domain names as gifts to minority-led nonprofits. These domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ give your organization a professional web address. We also provide guidance on connecting your domain to website builders and email services.

Who qualifies as a minority nonprofit?

We support nonprofits led by or primarily serving minority communities including Black, Hispanic/Latinx, Asian, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, immigrant, disabled, and other underrepresented groups. Organizations don't need 501(c)(3) status—community-based organizations also qualify.

How does the technology support work?

Apply through our website with information about your organization. Our team reviews applications and matches qualifying nonprofits with premium domains from our portfolio. Once approved, the domain transfers to your full ownership. We also provide setup guidance and resource recommendations.
