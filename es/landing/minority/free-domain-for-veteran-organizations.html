Free Domain for Veteran Organizations

Veteran organizations can receive free premium domains worth $500-$100,000+.

Supporting Veteran Digital Presence

Veteran-serving organizations provide crucial support—from mental health services and housing assistance to job training and community connection. A professional digital presence helps these organizations reach veterans who need their services.

Many veteran organizations, especially grassroots groups and those serving underrepresented veteran populations, struggle to establish professional online presence. A premium domain builds trust with both veterans seeking help and potential supporters.

We Build Pilares supports organizations serving veteran communities, with priority given to those focused on underrepresented populations. We gift premium domains to help build the digital infrastructure these vital organizations deserve.

Preguntas Frecuentes

Can veteran organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We support organizations serving veterans, particularly those focused on underrepresented veteran populations. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying veteran-serving organizations.

What types of veteran organizations qualify?

We support veteran advocacy groups, transition assistance programs, mental health services, homeless veteran programs, veteran arts organizations, support groups, and any nonprofit primarily serving military veterans and their families.

Do you prioritize certain veteran organizations?

We give priority to organizations serving underrepresented veteran populations including women veterans, LGBTQ+ veterans, veterans of color, disabled veterans, and homeless veterans.
