Digital Equity Programs

Building real digital wealth for underserved communities through premium domain gifting.

What Is Digital Equity?

Digital equity isn't just about internet access. It's about having the digital assets, skills, and presence needed to fully participate in today's economy and society.

Most digital equity initiatives focus on connectivity—broadband access, devices, digital literacy training. These are essential. But they miss a critical piece: digital assets.

Premium domain names are digital real estate. Like physical real estate, they're scarce, valuable, and appreciate over time. And like physical real estate, they've historically been accumulated by those with capital to invest—leaving underserved communities without these foundational digital assets.

We Build Pilares addresses this gap. We gift premium domains—worth $500 to $100,000+—to organizations led by or serving pillar communities. This is digital equity in action: redistributing digital wealth to create more equitable opportunity.

Our Digital Equity Approach

Asset Transfer

We don't loan or license domains—we transfer full ownership. Recipients control their digital assets completely, building wealth that stays in their communities.

Premium Quality

We gift truly valuable domains—memorable words and phrases—not leftover URLs. The same quality of digital assets that well-resourced organizations command.

Comunidad Priority

We specifically serve pillar communities: Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women-led, immigrant, disabled, and other historically underrepresented groups.

Capacity Building

Beyond the domain, higher tiers receive digital strategy support—helping organizations maximize the value of their new digital asset.

Communities We Serve

Brown & Latinx

Hispanic, Latino, and Brown-led organizations and community groups.

Indigenous Peoples

LGBTQ+ Communities

The Digital Equity Ecosystem

We Build Pilares is one part of a broader digital equity landscape. Here's how we fit:

  • Connectivity Programs — Broadband access initiatives, subsidized internet (we complement these)
  • Device Programs — Computer and device donations (we complement these)
  • Digital Literacy — Training and education programs (we complement these)
  • Software Access — Programs like TechSoup, Google for Organizaciones Sin Fines de Lucro (we complement these)
  • Digital Assets — Premium domain gifting (this is our focus)

While other programs help communities get online and use digital tools, we focus on building permanent digital wealth through asset ownership.

Preguntas Frecuentes

What is digital equity?

Digital equity is the condition where all individuals and communities have the technology capacity needed for full participation in society. This includes not just internet access, but digital assets, skills, and presence that enable economic and social opportunity.

How do digital equity programs help underserved communities?

Digital equity programs provide resources that bridge the technology gap: internet access, devices, training, and digital assets. We Build Pilares focuses on digital asset equity—gifting premium domains that would otherwise cost $500-$100,000+ to organizations serving marginalized communities.

Why are premium domains part of digital equity?

Premium domains are digital real estate—scarce, valuable assets that appreciate over time. Historically, these assets have been accumulated by those with capital to invest. By gifting premium domains to underserved communities, we're redistributing digital wealth to create more equitable opportunity.

Who qualifies for We Build Pilares' digital equity program?

Organizations led by or primarily serving pillar communities: Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women, immigrants, refugees, differently abled, neurodiverse, and other historically underrepresented groups. This includes nonprofits, social enterprises, artists, educators, and community organizations.
