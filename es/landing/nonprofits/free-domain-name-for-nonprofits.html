Preguntas Frecuentes

How can I get a free domain name for my nonprofit?

Apply through We Build Pilares by submitting a simple application explaining your mission and community impact. If your nonprofit is led by or serves underrepresented communities (Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women-led, differently abled, etc.), you may qualify for a premium domain gift valued between $500 and $100,000+.

What types of domain names are available for free to nonprofits?

We gift premium .org, .net, .com, and specialty TLD domains. These are memorable, brandable domain names that establish instant credibility—not random strings of characters, but real words and phrases that align with your mission.

Do I need 501(c)(3) status to get a free nonprofit domain?

While 501(c)(3) status is helpful, it's not required. We also support grassroots organizations, social enterprises, mutual aid networks, and community groups working toward formal nonprofit status. The key qualification is that your organization is led by or primarily serves pillar communities.

What is the value of the free domains offered to nonprofits?

Our premium domain gifts range from $500 to over $100,000 in market value. These aren't leftover domains—they're carefully curated, brandable names that would typically cost organizations significant resources to acquire.

Are there any ongoing costs after receiving a free domain?

We cover domain registration for the initial period. After that, you'll be responsible for the annual renewal fee (typically $10-20/year for standard domains). We'll help you understand what's needed and provide guidance on affordable hosting options.
