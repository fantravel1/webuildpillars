How to Get a Free Domain for Your Nonprofit

A straightforward guide to securing a premium domain for your mission-driven organization.

The Short Answer

We Build Pilares gifts premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying nonprofit organizations. If your nonprofit is led by or serves underrepresented communities, you can apply in under 10 minutes.

Unlike typical "free domain" offers that give you forgettable URLs or require expensive hosting packages, we provide truly premium domains—real words, memorable phrases, brandable names—transferred to your organization at no cost.

Step-by-Step: Getting Your Free Domain

Step 1: Check If You Qualify

We prioritize nonprofits led by or primarily serving pillar communities:

  • Black-led or Black-serving organizations
  • Brown and Latinx communities
  • Indigenous peoples and nations
  • LGBTQ+ and queer communities
  • Women-led organizations
  • Immigrant and refugee communities
  • Differently abled individuals
  • Neurodiverse communities
  • Other historically underrepresented groups

You don't need 501(c)(3) status. Grassroots organizations, mutual aid networks, and groups working toward formal nonprofit status may also qualify.

Step 2: Prepare Your Information

Before applying, have ready:

  • Organization Name — Your official or working name
  • Mission Statement — A brief description of what you do and why
  • Comunidad Impact — Who you serve and how
  • Current Website — If you have one (even a social media page counts)
  • Domain Preferences — Optional: any specific domain ideas you have

Step 3: Submit Your Application

Our application takes about 5-10 minutes. We ask for:

  • Basic contact information
  • Organization type (nonprofit, social enterprise, grassroots, etc.)
  • Communities you identify with and serve
  • Your mission and how a domain would help

Start Your Application →

Step 4: Wait for Review

Our team reviews applications within 2-4 weeks. We assess:

  • Alignment with pillar communities
  • Clarity of mission and impact
  • Domain availability in our portfolio
  • Best match between your mission and available domains

Step 5: Receive Your Domain

If approved, we'll contact you with domain options matched to your mission. Once you select your domain:

  • We initiate the domain transfer to your registrar account
  • Transfer typically completes in 1-2 weeks
  • You own the domain completely—full control of DNS, email, everything
  • Higher tiers receive additional support like email setup and hosting guidance

Preguntas Frecuentes

How do I get a free domain name for my nonprofit?

Apply through We Build Pilares by submitting a brief application about your organization and mission. If your nonprofit is led by or serves underrepresented communities, you may qualify for a premium domain gift valued at $500-$100,000+. The process takes 2-4 weeks.

What do I need to apply for a free nonprofit domain?

You'll need to describe your organization's mission, the communities you serve, and how a premium domain would help your work. Having 501(c)(3) status helps but isn't required. Grassroots organizations and groups working toward formal status may also qualify.

How long does it take to get a free nonprofit domain?

Most applications are reviewed within 2-4 weeks. If approved, domain transfer typically completes within 1-2 additional weeks. Overall, expect 3-6 weeks from application to having your domain active.

Is there a catch to getting a free nonprofit domain?

No catch. We Build Pilares is the social impact arm of Pillar Media & Entertainment. We gift premium domains as part of our mission to build digital equity. We ask only that you use the domain for legitimate nonprofit purposes and maintain active use.

What if my application is not approved?

If we can't approve your initial application, we'll explain why and may suggest alternative resources. You're welcome to reapply if circumstances change or if you can provide additional information about your community impact.

Ready to Apply?

It takes just 5-10 minutes to submit your application. Start the process now and take the first step toward a premium digital presence.
