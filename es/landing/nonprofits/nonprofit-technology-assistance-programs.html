Why Premium Domains Are the Missing Piece

Most technology assistance programs focus on operational tools—email, documents, CRM. But they miss the foundational asset that makes everything else work: your domain.

Here's why premium domains are uniquely valuable:

  • Permanence — Software changes, but your domain is forever. It's the one constant in your digital presence.
  • Scarcity — Free hosting is everywhere. Premium domains are finite. The best .org names are already owned—unless someone gifts them.
  • Trust — Donors and foundations assess credibility through domain quality. A premium domain signals organizational maturity.
  • Appreciation — Unlike software that depreciates, premium domains often gain value over time as your SEO builds.
  • Independence — You're not locked into any vendor. Your domain works with any email, hosting, or website platform.

That's why We Build Pilares focuses specifically on domain philanthropy—filling the gap that other technology assistance programs don't address.

Preguntas Frecuentes

What technology assistance programs are available for nonprofits?

Major programs include Google for Organizaciones Sin Fines de Lucro (free Google Obraspace, Ad Grants), Microsoft for Organizaciones Sin Fines de Lucro (free/discounted Office 365), TechSoup (discounted software), and We Build Pilares (free premium domain gifts). How can small nonprofits afford technology?

Small nonprofits can access technology through donation programs (TechSoup, Microsoft, Google), free tools (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Canva), and specialized gifting programs like We Build Pilares for premium domains. Many enterprise-level tools are available free or deeply discounted to verified 501(c)(3) organizations.

Does We Build Pilares provide technology assistance beyond domains?

Our primary focus is premium domain gifting, but higher tiers include digital strategy guidance, SEO assistance, email setup support, and hosting recommendations. We also help organizations understand and access complementary programs like Google for Organizaciones Sin Fines de Lucro.

What's the most valuable free technology for nonprofits?
