Preguntas Frecuentes

Can environmental groups get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares supports environmental and conservation organizations, especially those led by or serving underrepresented communities disproportionately affected by environmental issues. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What types of environmental groups qualify?

We support environmental justice organizations, conservation groups, climate advocacy, community gardens, urban farms, land trusts, and nature education programs. Priority goes to groups led by affected communities.

Why does We Build Pilares support environmental groups?

Environmental issues disproportionately affect pillar communities. Environmental justice is social justice, and we support organizations fighting for healthy environments in underrepresented communities.
