Preguntas Frecuentes

Can youth programs get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares supports youth-serving organizations, especially those serving young people from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What types of youth programs qualify?

We support after-school programs, youth development organizations, mentoring programs, youth arts programs, coding bootcamps for kids, summer camps, and youth advocacy organizations.

Can youth-led organizations apply?

Absolutely. We especially encourage applications from youth-led organizations where young people themselves are leading the work.
