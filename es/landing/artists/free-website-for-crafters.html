Preguntas Frecuentes

Can crafters get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares supports crafters and makers from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to help artisans build professional online presence beyond marketplace platforms.

What types of crafters qualify?

We support ceramicists, fiber artists, woodworkers, jewelry makers, leather workers, printmakers, and makers of all craft disciplines. Priority goes to makers from underrepresented communities practicing traditional or contemporary crafts.

Can I use this for an e-commerce site?

Yes. Your domain can host a portfolio, an online shop, or both. Full ownership means you control how the site functions.
