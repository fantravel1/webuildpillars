Free Domain for Musicians

Own your digital stage. Premium domains for musicians from underrepresented communities.

Your Music, Your Domain

Streaming platforms keep most of the money. Social media controls who sees your posts. But a domain you own? That's your permanent digital stage that no one can take away.

We Build Pilares gifts premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to musicians from underrepresented communities. A memorable domain like YourBandName.com becomes your home base: the place you send fans, the address on your EPK, the hub that ties everything together.

Why Musicians Need Their Own Domain

Direct-to-Fan Sales

Sell music, merch, and tickets directly—keep more of the revenue without platform cuts.

Email List Control

Build your mailing list on your domain. Social algorithms can't throttle email.

Professional EPK

Send venues and press to a professional URL, not a Linktree or social profile.

Platform Independence

When platforms change or disappear, your domain stays. Build for the long run.

Preguntas Frecuentes

Can musicians get free professional domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares gifts premium domains to musicians from underrepresented communities. A domain like YourBandName.com or ArtistName.music establishes your professional presence independent of streaming platforms and social media.

Why do musicians need their own domain?

Streaming platforms and social media change constantly. Your own domain is permanent infrastructure you control. It's where you can sell merch directly, collect email signups, and build a fanbase that no algorithm can take away.

Can bands apply together?

Yes. Bands and musical groups can apply for domains. The domain can be registered to the band as an entity or to a representative member.
