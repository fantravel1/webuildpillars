Preguntas Frecuentes

Can podcasters get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares supports podcasters and audio creators from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to help podcasters build professional show websites.

What types of podcasters qualify?

We support interview podcasters, narrative podcasters, audio journalists, audio fiction creators, and educational podcasters. Priority goes to creators from underrepresented communities whose voices are missing from mainstream audio.

Should I get a domain for my show or myself?

Either works. You might want a show-specific domain if you plan multiple shows, or a personal domain to brand yourself as a creator. Apply for whichever fits your goals.
