Free Domain for Dancers

Dancers can receive free premium domains worth $500-$100,000+.

Supporting Dancer Digital Presence

Dancers need professional platforms to showcase their movement, share performance videos, and connect with companies, venues, and audiences. A professional domain establishes you as a serious artist in the dance world.

Dancers from underrepresented communities often struggle to gain visibility in classical and contemporary dance spaces. A premium domain helps establish your presence and creates a hub for your artistic work.

We Build Pilares gifts premium domains to dancers from pillar communities, helping build the digital infrastructure that supports diverse movement artists.

Preguntas Frecuentes

Can dancers get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pilares supports dancers and choreographers from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to help dancers build professional online presence.

What types of dancers qualify?

We support ballet dancers, contemporary dancers, hip-hop artists, traditional cultural dancers, choreographers, dance educators, and dance companies. Priority goes to dancers from underrepresented communities.

Can our dance company apply?

Yes. Both individual dancers and dance companies/collectives can apply for premium domain gifts.
