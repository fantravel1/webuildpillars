Frequently Asked Questions

Can cooperatives get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars supports cooperatives and worker-owned businesses, especially those owned by workers from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What types of cooperatives qualify?

We support worker cooperatives, consumer cooperatives, producer cooperatives, housing cooperatives, and cooperative development organizations. Priority goes to cooperatives with diverse worker-ownership.
