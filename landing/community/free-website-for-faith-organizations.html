Can faith organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars supports faith-based organizations, especially those serving underrepresented communities or led by clergy from pillar communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What types of faith organizations qualify?

We support churches, mosques, synagogues, temples, interfaith organizations, faith-based service providers, and religious community centers. We welcome applications from all faith traditions.

No. We support faith-based organizations across all religious traditions—Christian, Muslim, Jewish, Buddhist, Hindu, Indigenous spiritual traditions, and all others. Faith communities are pillars of their communities.
