Frequently Asked Questions

Can mutual aid groups get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars supports mutual aid networks and community support groups. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to help mutual aid efforts build sustainable infrastructure.

Do we need to be a formal nonprofit?

No. Mutual aid often operates outside formal nonprofit structures, and that's okay. We support grassroots groups, informal networks, and community-based initiatives.

What makes mutual aid different from charity?

Mutual aid is solidarity, not charity—community members supporting each other based on shared need. We believe mutual aid networks deserve professional infrastructure to coordinate their essential work.
