How to Get a Professional Domain as an Artist

A step-by-step guide to receiving a premium domain gift.

Your Professional Domain Awaits

A memorable domain is essential for any serious artist. But premium domains—the short, memorable ones that stick in people's minds—can cost hundreds or thousands of dollars.

We Build Pillars removes that barrier by gifting premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to artists from underrepresented communities. Here's exactly how to get yours.

5 Steps to Your Free Premium Domain

1

Check Your Eligibility

Confirm you're part of an underrepresented community: Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women, immigrant, disabled, or neurodiverse. You should have an active artistic practice, whether emerging or established.

2

Prepare Your Materials

Gather examples of your work (portfolio, Instagram, or uploaded images), a brief description of your artistic practice, and any existing online presence. No formal resume required.

3

Submit Your Application

Complete our domain request form with information about yourself, your work, and how a premium domain would help your artistic career. The application takes about 10 minutes.

4

Application Review

Our team reviews your application and identifies premium domains from our portfolio that match your name, artistic practice, or brand. This typically takes 2-4 weeks.

5

Domain Transfer

Once approved, we transfer full ownership of the domain to you. It's yours completely—no strings, no ongoing fees to us, no reporting requirements. You own it outright.

Tips for a Strong Application

  • Show your work: Include links to your portfolio, Instagram, or upload images. We want to see what you create.
  • Explain your vision: Tell us how you'll use the domain. What will you build with it?
  • Be specific about impact: How will a professional domain help your artistic career?
  • Community connection: Describe how your work connects to or serves underrepresented communities.

You don't need to be a famous artist. We support creatives at all stages—from emerging artists building their first portfolio to established professionals seeking a better domain.

Frequently Asked Questions

What domains are available for artists?

We have premium domains across many extensions including .com, .art, .design, .studio, .photo, .gallery, and more. We'll match you with a domain that fits your name or artistic brand.

Can I request a specific domain?

You can indicate preferences in your application, and we'll check if matching domains are available in our portfolio. We can't guarantee specific domains, but we'll work to find the best match.

What if I'm just starting out?

We support artists at all career stages. Emerging artists often benefit most from a premium domain—it establishes professional credibility from the beginning of your career.
