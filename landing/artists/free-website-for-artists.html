Own Your Digital Gallery

Social media giveth and social media taketh away. Algorithms change. Platforms rise and fall. But a domain you own? That's yours forever.

We Build Pillars gifts premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to artists from underrepresented communities. A memorable domain like YourName.art or YourStudio.com becomes your permanent digital home, building authority and recognition that social profiles can never match.

Whether you're a visual artist, musician, writer, filmmaker, or any kind of creative, a professional domain establishes credibility with galleries, collectors, clients, and collaborators.

Why Artists Need Premium Domains

Gallery-Ready Presence

When galleries and curators research you, yourname.art signals professionalism. It's your first impression before they see your work.

Platform Independence

Instagram bans, algorithm changes, platform shutdowns—none of it affects your own domain. Your portfolio stays accessible no matter what.

Professional Email

Reach out to galleries as hello@yourname.art instead of a Gmail address. Small difference, big impact.

SEO That Compounds

Every year you own your domain, it builds search authority. Collectors searching for you will find your site first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can artists get free premium domains for their portfolios?

Yes. We Build Pillars gifts premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to artists from underrepresented communities. A professional domain like YourName.art or YourStudio.com establishes credibility with galleries, collectors, and clients.

What types of artists qualify for free domains?

Visual artists, musicians, writers, filmmakers, performers, photographers, and all other creative practitioners from pillar communities. The key qualification is being part of an underrepresented community: Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women, immigrant, differently abled, or neurodiverse.

Why is a premium domain important for artists?

Your domain is your permanent digital gallery. Unlike social media profiles that can disappear or change algorithms, a domain you own gives you complete control. A premium domain is memorable, builds SEO over time, and signals professionalism to galleries, clients, and collectors.

Do I need to have gallery representation to qualify?

No. We support artists at all stages of their careers—emerging, mid-career, and established. What matters is your creative practice and your connection to pillar communities.
