Frequently Asked Questions

What technology resources are available for minority entrepreneurs?

Key resources include: We Build Pillars (free premium domains), Google for Startups, Microsoft for Startups, various accelerator programs, and SBA resources. We Build Pillars specifically focuses on gifting premium domains—valuable digital assets—to minority-led social enterprises and mission-driven businesses.

Can minority-owned businesses get free domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars gifts premium domains to minority entrepreneurs running social enterprises or mission-driven businesses. If your business serves your community or has social impact beyond profit, you may qualify for a domain valued at $500 to $100,000+.

I'm a for-profit business. Do I qualify?

We focus on social enterprises and mission-driven businesses, not purely profit-focused companies. However, if your business has significant community benefit or social impact components, you may still qualify. Apply and share your story.
