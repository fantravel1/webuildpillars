Premium domain gifting for organizations led by women or serving women and girls.

Empowering Women's Digital Leadership

Women-led organizations drive change across every sector—healthcare, education, advocacy, arts, and community development. Yet these organizations often operate with fewer resources than their counterparts.

A premium domain is a strategic asset that establishes credibility from day one. When a women's shelter operates from SafeHaven.org rather than a free subdomain, donors take notice. Foundations see organizational maturity. And women seeking help find a presence they can trust.

We Build Pillars recognizes women-led and female/non-binary-led organizations as a priority pillar community. We gift premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to organizations led by women or primarily serving women and girls.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can women-led organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars includes women-led and female/non-binary-led organizations as priority pillar communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying organizations led by women or serving women and girls.

What types of women's organizations qualify?

We support women-led nonprofits, organizations serving women and girls, women's health organizations, domestic violence shelters, girls' education programs, women's professional networks, female entrepreneurship programs, and maternal/reproductive health organizations.

Do non-binary-led organizations also qualify?

Yes. Our pillar community definition includes female and non-binary-led organizations. We recognize the shared challenges of underrepresentation and support organizations across the gender spectrum.

What if our organization serves women but isn't women-led?

Organizations that primarily serve women and girls qualify even if current leadership isn't all-female. The key is mission alignment with supporting and empowering women.
