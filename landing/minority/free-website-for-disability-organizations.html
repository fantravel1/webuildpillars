Supporting Disability Digital Presence

Disability-led organizations do essential work—from advocacy and independent living support to arts programs and community building. A professional digital presence helps these organizations reach people who need their services and resources.

Disability organizations often face unique funding challenges while serving communities that depend on accessible digital resources. A professional domain establishes credibility and provides a foundation for building truly accessible online experiences.

We Build Pillars recognizes differently abled communities as a priority pillar community. We gift premium domains to organizations serving disabled individuals, helping build the digital infrastructure these vital organizations deserve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can disability organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars includes differently abled communities as a priority pillar community. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to disability-led organizations and groups serving people with disabilities.

What types of disability organizations qualify?

We support disability advocacy organizations, accessibility services, independent living centers, adaptive recreation programs, disability arts organizations, support groups, and any nonprofit primarily serving people with disabilities.

Will my website need to be accessible?

We encourage all recipients to build accessible websites, and we can provide guidance on web accessibility best practices. However, accessibility requirements are not a condition of receiving a domain.
