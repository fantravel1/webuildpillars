Frequently Asked Questions

Can immigrant organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars recognizes immigrant and migrant communities as priority pillar communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying immigrant-led organizations and groups serving immigrant communities.

What types of immigrant organizations qualify?

We support immigrant advocacy groups, cultural associations, legal aid organizations, resettlement agencies, language programs, immigrant worker centers, and any nonprofit primarily serving immigrant communities.

Do we need to be a registered nonprofit?

No. We accept applications from registered nonprofits, grassroots organizations, community groups, and mutual aid networks. The key is mission alignment and community service, not formal nonprofit status.
