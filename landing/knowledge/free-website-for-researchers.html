Free Website for Researchers

Premium domains for sharing knowledge with the world.

Your Research Deserves Visibility

Research locked behind paywalls and institutional barriers fails to reach the communities that need it most. A professional website for your research changes that.

We Build Pillars gifts premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to researchers from underrepresented communities. A domain like YourResearch.org or YourLab.org gives your work a permanent, professional home on the internet.

Share findings, host datasets, build collaborations, and reach both academic and public audiences. Your research has impact—let's give it the platform it deserves.

Lab or Center

Establish a web presence for your research lab, center, or collaborative group.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can researchers get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars gifts premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to researchers from underrepresented communities. A professional domain helps disseminate research, build credibility with funders, and create a lasting academic presence.

What types of research projects qualify?

We support academic researchers, independent scholars, research labs, think tanks, and research-focused nonprofits. Projects can span any discipline—sciences, humanities, social sciences, arts research, and more.

Do I need university affiliation?

No. We support both academic and independent researchers. Community-based research, independent scholarship, and research conducted outside traditional institutions all qualify.
