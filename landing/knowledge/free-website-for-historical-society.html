Free Website for Historical Society

Premium domains for organizations preserving community history.

Your History Deserves a Permanent Home

Historical societies preserve the stories that define communities. But many operate with outdated websites or no web presence at all, limiting their reach and impact.

A professional domain like YourHistoricalSociety.org signals permanence and credibility. It's the address researchers cite, journalists link to, and community members remember.

We Build Pillars gifts premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to historical societies preserving the stories of underrepresented communities. Build your digital presence on a foundation as lasting as the history you protect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can historical societies get free domains?

Yes. We Build Pillars gifts premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to historical societies that preserve the stories of underrepresented communities. A professional domain helps your society establish credibility and reach wider audiences.

What should a historical society website include?

Key elements include: your mission and history, digital collections and archives, upcoming events and programs, membership information, research resources, contact information, and ways to donate or volunteer.

Do we need to be a large organization?

No. We support historical societies and heritage organizations of all sizes—from small neighborhood history groups to larger regional organizations. The key is that you preserve the history of underrepresented communities.
