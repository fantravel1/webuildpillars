Digital Resources for Nonprofits

Essential tools and assets for building a professional online presence—mostly free.

Building Your Nonprofit's Digital Foundation

Every nonprofit needs a professional digital presence. The good news: most essential digital resources are available free or heavily discounted for mission-driven organizations.

The challenge isn't access—it's knowing what to prioritize. This guide covers essential digital resources, starting with the most foundational: your domain name.

Essential Digital Resources by Category

Domain Name

Your digital address and most permanent asset. We Build Pillars gifts premium domains ($500–$100,000+ value) to qualifying nonprofits from pillar communities.

Email & Productivity

Google Workspace (free via Google for Nonprofits) or Microsoft 365 (free/discounted via Microsoft for Nonprofits). Professional email + docs + collaboration.

Website & Hosting

Free options: GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare Pages. Website builders: Wix, Squarespace (nonprofit discounts), WordPress. Google Sites free with Workspace.

Design Tools

Canva for Nonprofits (free Pro access) for graphics, social media, and marketing materials. Figma also offers nonprofit programs.

Communication

Slack (85% nonprofit discount), Microsoft Teams (free with 365), Zoom (nonprofit discounts), Google Meet (free with Workspace).

Fundraising & CRM

Donorbox, Little Green Light, Bloomerang, and Salesforce (nonprofit program) offer various pricing for donor management and online giving.

Start with Your Domain: Here's Why

Your domain is the one digital resource that everything else depends on. Get this right first.

  • Email Depends on It — You can't have professional email (team@yourmission.org) without your own domain
  • Website Anchors to It — Whatever platform you choose, your domain is the consistent address
  • Marketing Points to It — Business cards, social profiles, and campaigns all reference your domain
  • It's Permanent — Software changes; your domain stays with you forever
  • It Builds Value — SEO authority compounds over time on your domain
  • It's Scarce — Unlike free hosting, premium domains are limited. The best ones are already owned.

That's why We Build Pillars focuses on domain gifting. We fill the gap that other nonprofit resource programs miss.

Frequently Asked Questions

What digital resources are essential for nonprofits?

Essential digital resources include: a professional domain name, email hosting, website or web presence, design tools, communication platforms, and donor management systems. Most of these are available free or discounted through various nonprofit programs.

Where can nonprofits get free digital resources?

Key sources include: We Build Pillars (free premium domains), Google for Nonprofits (Workspace, Ad Grants), Microsoft for Nonprofits (Office 365), TechSoup (discounted software), Canva for Nonprofits, and various free hosting platforms like GitHub Pages and Netlify.

What's the most important digital asset for a nonprofit?

Your domain name is the most important digital asset because it's permanent, scarce, and foundational. All other digital tools connect to your domain—email, website, marketing. Premium domains build value over time and establish credibility with donors and foundations.

How do I know if my nonprofit qualifies for free resources?

Most major programs require 501(c)(3) status. TechSoup verification is often used as proof. We Build Pillars also accepts grassroots organizations and those working toward nonprofit status—the key is serving pillar communities. Each program has specific eligibility requirements on their website.
