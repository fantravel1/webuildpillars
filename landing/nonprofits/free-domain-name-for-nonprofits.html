Yes, You Can Get a Free Premium Domain

We Build Pillars gifts premium domain names—valued between $500 and $100,000+—to qualifying nonprofit organizations. No catch. No hidden fees. Just a genuine investment in your mission.

Unlike typical "free domain" offers that give you forgettable, hyphenated URLs or force you into expensive hosting packages, we provide truly valuable domain names: real words, memorable phrases, and brandable addresses that establish instant credibility.

Our domain portfolio includes thousands of premium .org, .net, .com, and specialty TLD domains. When you apply, we work to match you with a domain that aligns with your mission and amplifies your impact.

Why a Premium Domain Matters for Your Nonprofit

Build Donor Trust

89% of donors check an organization's website before giving. A premium domain like YourMission.org signals legitimacy and stability far better than YourMission.wixsite.com.

Professional Email

Stop using Gmail or Yahoo for donor outreach. A domain gives you team@yourmission.org—emails that stay out of spam folders and build professional credibility.

Search Visibility

How to Get Your Free Domain

Getting a free premium domain for your nonprofit is straightforward. Here's the process:

  1. Submit Your Application — Tell us about your organization, your mission, and the communities you serve. Include any current website or social media presence.
  2. We Review & Match — Our team reviews applications and matches qualifying organizations with appropriate domains from our portfolio based on mission alignment and availability.
  3. Receive Your Domain — Once matched, we transfer the domain to you completely free. Depending on your tier, you may also receive email setup assistance, hosting guidance, or ongoing support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get a free domain name for my nonprofit?

Apply through We Build Pillars by submitting a simple application explaining your mission and community impact. If your nonprofit is led by or serves underrepresented communities (Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women-led, differently abled, etc.), you may qualify for a premium domain gift valued between $500 and $100,000+.

What types of domain names are available for free to nonprofits?

We gift premium .org, .net, .com, and specialty TLD domains. These are memorable, brandable domain names that establish instant credibility—not random strings of characters, but real words and phrases that align with your mission.

Do I need 501(c)(3) status to get a free nonprofit domain?

While 501(c)(3) status is helpful, it's not required. We also support grassroots organizations, social enterprises, mutual aid networks, and community groups working toward formal nonprofit status. The key qualification is that your organization is led by or primarily serves pillar communities.

What is the value of the free domains offered to nonprofits?

Our premium domain gifts range from $500 to over $100,000 in market value. These aren't leftover domains—they're carefully curated, brandable names that would typically cost organizations significant resources to acquire.

Are there any ongoing costs after receiving a free domain?

We cover domain registration for the initial period. After that, you'll be responsible for the annual renewal fee (typically $10-20/year for standard domains). We'll help you understand what's needed and provide guidance on affordable hosting options.
