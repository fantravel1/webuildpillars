Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our domain gifting program.

General Questions

What is We Build Pillars?

We Build Pillars is the social impact arm of Pillar Media & Entertainment. We gift premium domains from our $260M+ portfolio to nonprofits, artists, and knowledge builders from pillar communities—those who are Black, Brown, Queer, Indigenous, neurodiverse, female, migrant, or differently abled.

We believe digital identity shouldn't be determined by budget. A premium domain creates credibility, trust, and opportunity that can transform how an organization or individual is perceived.

Is this really free?

Yes, the domain itself is completely free. There is no cost to receive a premium domain through our program.

You will be responsible for ongoing costs like:

  • Web hosting services (if you build a website)
  • Annual domain renewal fees (typically $10-20/year for standard TLDs)
  • Email hosting (if you want professional email)

But the domain—which may be valued at thousands or even tens of thousands of dollars—is gifted at no cost to you.

What's the catch?

There's no catch. We're not selling anything, and we don't take equity or require ongoing payments.

Our motivation is simple: we have valuable digital assets (premium domains), and we believe deploying them to mission-driven organizations creates more value than sitting on them or selling to the highest bidder.

When pillar communities thrive, we all thrive. This is strategic philanthropy, not charity with strings attached.

Eligibility

Who qualifies for a domain?

We prioritize organizations and individuals from pillar communities working in three areas:

  1. Nonprofits and social enterprises creating community impact
  2. Artists and creators building their professional presence
  3. Knowledge builders including educators, researchers, and archivists

You don't need to be a registered nonprofit—individual creators and grassroots groups are welcome to apply.

What are "pillar communities"?

Pillar communities are those who form the foundational support of our society, yet are often underrepresented and under-resourced in the digital economy. This includes people who are:

  • Black
  • Brown / Latinx
  • Indigenous
  • LGBTQ+ / Queer
  • Neurodiverse
  • Female / Non-binary
  • Migrant / Immigrant
  • Differently abled

We chose "pillar" deliberately—these communities aren't marginalized; they're foundational.

I'm an individual, not an organization. Can I apply?

Absolutely. Individual artists, creators, educators, and knowledge builders are welcome to apply. Your personal brand and professional identity matter just as much as organizational ones.

Many of our domains are perfect for individuals building their creative careers or thought leadership platforms.

Do I need to be in the United States?

No. We support pillar communities worldwide. While our team is US-based, our domain gifting program has no geographic restrictions.

International applicants are welcome, though some communication may need to happen in English.

The Process

How long does the process take?

From application to domain transfer typically takes 2-4 weeks:

  • Application review: 5-7 business days
  • Follow-up conversation: Scheduled within 1 week of approval
  • Domain matching and selection: 1-2 weeks
  • Transfer: 2-5 business days

Timeline may vary based on domain availability and your responsiveness to our communications.

Can I request a specific domain?

You can indicate preferences in your application, but we match from our existing portfolio rather than acquiring new domains.

We'll present options that align with your mission and brand—you may discover an even better fit than you originally imagined. Our portfolio includes over 12,000 premium domains across various TLDs.

What are the commitment tiers?

We offer four tiers of support, all free:

  • Foundation: Premium domain + basic DNS setup guidance
  • Growth: Domain + email configuration + hosting recommendations
  • Impact: Full digital identity support including SEO and brand alignment
  • Legacy: Strategic partnership with ongoing mentorship

The tier is determined based on your needs, readiness, and the scope of your work. Higher tiers typically involve higher-value domains and more intensive support.

What happens after I receive a domain?

Once you select and accept a domain, we transfer full ownership to you. The domain becomes yours permanently—there are no usage restrictions or take-back clauses.

Depending on your tier, you'll receive varying levels of ongoing support. You're responsible for using the domain to further your mission, maintaining hosting and renewals, and building something meaningful with your new digital identity.

Technical Questions

Do I need technical skills?

No technical skills are required to apply or receive a domain. Our Growth, Impact, and Legacy tiers include guidance on:

  • DNS setup and configuration
  • Email setup (Google Workspace, etc.)
  • Hosting provider selection
  • Website building options

We meet you where you are and provide the support you need to make the most of your new domain.

Who handles hosting and email?

You're responsible for selecting and paying for your own hosting and email services. We provide guidance and recommendations, but don't provide hosting ourselves.

This ensures you maintain full control and aren't dependent on us for ongoing services. Many affordable options exist—we'll help you find what fits your needs and budget.

What registrar will the domain be at?

We can transfer the domain to your preferred registrar, or help you set up an account at a reputable registrar like Cloudflare, Namecheap, or Google Domains.

You'll have full ownership and control through your own registrar account.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 2 business days.
