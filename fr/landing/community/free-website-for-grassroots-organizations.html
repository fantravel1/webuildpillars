Questions Fréquentes

Can grassroots organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers supports grassroots organizations and community-led initiatives. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to help grassroots groups build professional infrastructure.

What makes an organization 'grassroots'?

Grassroots organizations are community-led, emerging from and accountable to the communities they serve. They typically rely on volunteer energy and community funding rather than large grants.

Do we need formal nonprofit status?

No. Many grassroots efforts operate as informal groups, fiscally sponsored projects, or community-based initiatives. We support groups at all stages of formalization.
