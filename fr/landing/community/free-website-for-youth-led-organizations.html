Supporting Youth-Led Digital Presence

Young people are leading movements, starting organizations, and creating change. Youth-led organizations deserve the same professional infrastructure as adult-led groups—and often need it even more to be taken seriously.

Young leaders from underrepresented communities face additional barriers to being heard and respected. A premium domain helps youth-led organizations establish credibility with funders, partners, and the public.

We Build Piliers supports youth-led organizations in pillar communities, helping build the digital infrastructure for the next generation of changemakers.

Questions Fréquentes

Can youth-led organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers enthusiastically supports youth-led organizations where young people are in leadership. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What age qualifies as 'youth-led'?

We typically consider youth-led to mean primarily led by people under 25, but we're flexible. The key is that young people hold real leadership and decision-making power in the organization.

Can adult allies apply on behalf of youth?

Yes, but the application should reflect that young people are genuinely leading the work. Adult allies can support the application process while centering youth voices.
