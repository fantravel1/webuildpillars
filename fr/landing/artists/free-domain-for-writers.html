Free Domain for Writers

Writers can receive free premium domains worth $500-$100,000+.

Supporting Writer Digital Presence

Writers need professional platforms to share their work, connect with readers, and build literary careers. A professional domain establishes you as a serious author and provides a home for your writing that you control.

Writers from underrepresented communities often lack the resources for professional branding while facing additional barriers to publication and recognition. A premium domain like YourName.com provides the professional foundation you deserve.

We Build Piliers gifts premium domains to writers from pillar communities, helping build the digital infrastructure that supports diverse literary voices.

Questions Fréquentes

Can writers get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers supports writers and authors from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to help writers build professional author platforms.

What types of writers qualify?

We support novelists, poets, essayists, journalists, playwrights, screenwriters, literary translators, and writers of all genres. Priority goes to writers from underrepresented communities including Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women, immigrant, and disabled writers.

Do I need to be published?

No. We support both published and emerging writers. What matters is your commitment to your craft and your connection to underrepresented communities.
