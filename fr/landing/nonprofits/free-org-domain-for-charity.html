The trusted extension for charitable organizations—gifted to those who need it most.

Why .org Is the Gold Standard for Charities

Since 1985, .org has been the trusted domain extension for organizations making a difference. When donors see a .org address, they instinctively know they're dealing with a mission-driven organization.

But not all .org domains are created equal. help-kids-now-charity-2024.org doesn't inspire the same confidence as HelpingKids.org. Premium .org domains—short, memorable, containing real words—can cost $500 to $100,000+ on the open market.

We Build Piliers gifts these premium .org domains to qualifying charities from underrepresented communities. No purchase required. No hidden fees. Just a genuine investment in your mission.

The .org Advantage for Charities

Instant Recognition

Donors, foundations, and partners recognize .org as the nonprofit standard. It's visual shorthand for "mission-driven organization."

Search Credibility

Search engines and users alike associate .org with trustworthy, non-commercial content. This perception boost helps your SEO and click-through rates.

Email Trust

Emails from director@yourcharity.org are more likely to be opened and less likely to hit spam filters than generic email addresses.

Long-term Value

A strong .org domain appreciates in value and brand equity over time. It's a permanent asset that anchors all your digital activities.

Premium .org vs. Standard .org: The Difference

Any charity can register a .org domain for about $10-15/year. The challenge is finding a good one:

HealingHands.org Premium ($5,000+ value)
healing-hands-charity.org Standard ($12/year)
HopeRising.org Premium ($3,000+ value)
hope-rising-org-2024.org Standard ($12/year)

We Build Piliers gifts the premium versions—domains that would otherwise cost hundreds or thousands of dollars—to charities from pillar communities.

Qui Est Éligible for a Free .org Domain?

We prioritize charities led by or serving pillar communities:

Registered Charities

501(c)(3) organizations and international equivalents with active charitable programs.

Religious Charities

Faith-based organizations providing charitable services to underserved communities.

Communauté Foundations

Local charitable foundations serving specific geographic or identity-based communities.

Charitable Programs

Projects within larger organizations that have distinct charitable missions.

Mutual Aid

Communauté-based charitable giving and support networks.

Emerging Charities

New organizations working toward formal charitable status.

Key requirement: Your charity must be led by or primarily serve pillar communities—Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women, immigrants, differently abled, or other historically underrepresented groups.

Questions Fréquentes

How can a charity get a free .org domain?

Apply through We Build Piliers. We gift premium .org domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying charitable organizations. Your charity should be led by or serve underrepresented communities to qualify.

Why is .org the best domain extension for charities?

The .org extension has been associated with nonprofits and charitable organizations since 1985. Donors instinctively trust .org addresses. A premium .org domain like HelpingHands.org signals legitimacy far more than a generic or hyphenated alternative.

Are all .org domains free for charities?

No. Standard .org registration costs about $10-15/year from any registrar. Premium .org domains—short, memorable, real words—can cost $500 to $100,000+ on the open market. We Build Piliers gifts these premium domains to qualifying charities.

What makes a .org domain "premium"?

Premium domains are short, memorable, and contain real words or phrases. Examples: HealingHands.org, HopeRising.org, CommunautéFirst.org. These domains are valuable because they're easy to remember, build trust instantly, and improve SEO.

Can I also get a .com or other extension?

Yes. While .org is ideal for charities, our portfolio also includes premium .net, .com, and specialty TLD domains. We'll match you with the best fit for your mission and brand.
