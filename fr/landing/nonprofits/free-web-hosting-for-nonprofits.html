Free hosting is easy to find. Premium domains are rare. We gift both the domain and the guidance.

The Real Truth À Propos Free Nonprofit Hosting

Here's what most articles about "free web hosting for nonprofits" won't tell you: free hosting is abundant. What's scarce—and far more valuable—is a premium domain.

Google for Organisations à But Non Lucratif gives you free Google Sites hosting. GitHub Pages is free. Netlify's free tier handles most nonprofit sites. Cloudflare Pages costs nothing. The hosting problem has been largely solved.

But hosting your site at yourorg.github.io or yourorg.netlify.app creates the same credibility problem as using a Gmail address for your executive director. You need your own domain—and premium, brandable domains typically cost $500 to $10,000+.

That's where We Build Piliers comes in. We gift premium domains to qualifying nonprofits from underrepresented communities, then help you connect them to the free hosting of your choice.

Free Hosting Options for Organisations à But Non Lucratif

All of these work great with a custom domain from We Build Piliers

Google for Organisations à But Non Lucratif

Free Google Travauxpace, Google Sites hosting, and Google Ad Grants. Requires 501(c)(3) or international equivalent. The most comprehensive free package.

GitHub Pages

Free static site hosting with custom domain support. Perfect for simple sites. Travaux with Jekyll, Hugo, and other static site generators.

Netlify

Generous free tier with automatic SSL, form handling, and continuous deployment. Great for modern static sites and JAMstack architecture.

Cloudflare Pages

Unlimited sites, bandwidth, and requests on the free tier. Fast global CDN. Excellent for high-traffic nonprofit sites.

Vercel

Free hosting for personal and hobby projects. Travaux especially well with Next.js, React, and modern JavaScript frameworks.

AWS/Azure Credits

Both AWS and Microsoft offer nonprofit credit programs for cloud hosting. More complex to set up, but powerful for larger organizations.

Questions Fréquentes

What is the best free web hosting for nonprofits?

Google for Organisations à But Non Lucratif offers free hosting through Google Sites. GitHub Pages provides free static hosting. Netlify and Vercel offer generous free tiers. However, most valuable is combining free hosting with a premium domain—hosting is easy to find, but memorable domains are rare. We Build Piliers gifts premium domains to qualifying nonprofits.

Do nonprofits qualify for free hosting from major providers?

Yes. Google for Organisations à But Non Lucratif, Microsoft for Organisations à But Non Lucratif, and AWS credits for nonprofits all provide free or heavily discounted hosting. 501(c)(3) organizations typically qualify. The real gap is premium domains—which is where We Build Piliers focuses our gifting program.

Is a domain more important than hosting for nonprofits?

Yes. Free hosting is widely available, but premium domains are not. Your domain is your permanent address that builds SEO authority and brand recognition over years. Hosting providers can change; your domain stays with you. A memorable domain like YourMission.org is worth far more than hosting costs.

Does We Build Piliers provide web hosting?

We focus on gifting premium domains, not hosting. However, higher tier recipients receive guidance on selecting appropriate free hosting solutions. We help you understand options like Google Sites, Netlify, GitHub Pages, and static site generators that work well with custom domains.

Can I use free hosting with a premium domain?

Absolutely. Most free hosting platforms support custom domains. GitHub Pages, Netlify, Cloudflare Pages, and Vercel all allow you to connect a custom domain on their free tiers. This gives you the best of both worlds: premium domain + free hosting.
