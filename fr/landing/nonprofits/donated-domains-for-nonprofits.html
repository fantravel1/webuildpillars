Donated Domains for Organisations à But Non Lucratif

Premium domain philanthropy for organizations creating real community impact.

Real Domain Donations, Not Just Discounts

Most "nonprofit domain programs" offer a few dollars off registration fees. We Build Piliers does something fundamentally different: we gift premium domains worth $500 to $100,000+ to qualifying organizations.

These aren't leftover domains nobody wanted. They're carefully curated, brandable names that would typically cost significant resources to acquire on the open market. Names that establish instant credibility. Names that donors remember.

Our domain philanthropy focuses on pillar communities—organizations led by or serving Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women, immigrants, differently abled, and other underrepresented groups who've historically been excluded from digital wealth.

How Our Domain Donations Work

True Premium Domains

We gift brandable, memorable domains—real words and meaningful phrases. Not random-string.org or your-name-here-2024.com.

Propriété Totale

The domain transfers to your organization completely. You control DNS, hosting, email—everything. It's truly yours.

Mission Matching

We don't just hand out random domains. Our team matches your mission with a domain that amplifies your work and resonates with your community.

Tiered Support

Beyond the domain, higher tiers include email setup, digital strategy guidance, and ongoing support to maximize your new digital asset.

Questions Fréquentes

Where can nonprofits get donated domain names?

We Build Piliers operates a domain gifting program specifically for nonprofits from underrepresented communities. Unlike typical domain discount programs, we gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ at no cost to qualifying organizations.

What's the difference between donated domains and discounted domains?

Most "nonprofit domain programs" offer discounts on registration fees (saving $10-15/year) or free basic domains. Donated premium domains from We Build Piliers are high-value, brandable names that would otherwise cost hundreds or thousands to purchase—given completely free.

How do domain donations work?

We Build Piliers maintains a portfolio of premium domains. When a nonprofit applies and qualifies, we match them with an appropriate domain and transfer full ownership to the organization. The nonprofit then owns the domain outright.

Do I fully own the donated domain?

Yes. Once transferred, the domain is yours. You control DNS, renewals, and all decisions. We simply ask that domains be used for legitimate nonprofit purposes and that you maintain active use.

What are the ongoing costs after receiving a donated domain?

We cover the initial registration period. After that, you'll be responsible for annual renewal fees, typically $10-20/year for standard TLDs. Premium domains may have higher renewal fees, which we'll disclose before transfer.
