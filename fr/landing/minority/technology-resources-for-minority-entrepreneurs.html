Digital Foundation for Minority-Owned Businesses

Minority entrepreneurs face unique challenges accessing technology resources. While many programs focus on funding and mentorship, few address the digital assets that establish business credibility from day one.

A premium domain is your business's permanent address. YourBrand.com establishes professionalism that yourbrand.squarespace.com simply cannot match. We Build Piliers gifts premium domains—worth $500 to $100,000+—to minority entrepreneurs building social enterprises and mission-driven businesses.

Questions Fréquentes

What technology resources are available for minority entrepreneurs?

Key resources include: We Build Piliers (free premium domains), Google for Startups, Microsoft for Startups, various accelerator programs, and SBA resources. We Build Piliers specifically focuses on gifting premium domains—valuable digital assets—to minority-led social enterprises and mission-driven businesses.

Can minority-owned businesses get free domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers gifts premium domains to minority entrepreneurs running social enterprises or mission-driven businesses. If your business serves your community or has social impact beyond profit, you may qualify for a domain valued at $500 to $100,000+.

I'm a for-profit business. Do I qualify?

We focus on social enterprises and mission-driven businesses, not purely profit-focused companies. However, if your business has significant community benefit or social impact components, you may still qualify. Apply and share your story.
