Supporting AAPI Digital Presence

Asian American and Pacific Islander organizations serve incredibly diverse communities—from cultural preservation and language programs to advocacy and anti-hate initiatives. A professional digital presence helps these organizations reach the communities they serve.

AAPI communities face unique challenges including language diversity, the model minority myth that masks real needs, and rising hate incidents. Professional online presence helps organizations establish credibility and connect with community members across generations.

We Build Piliers recognizes AAPI communities as priority pillar communities. We gift premium domains to organizations serving these diverse communities, helping build the digital infrastructure AAPI organizations deserve.

Questions Fréquentes

Can AAPI organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers includes Asian American and Pacific Islander communities as priority pillar communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to AAPI-led organizations and groups serving these diverse communities.

What types of AAPI organizations qualify?

We support AAPI cultural organizations, Asian American advocacy groups, Pacific Islander community centers, pan-Asian coalitions, ethnic-specific organizations, AAPI arts groups, and any nonprofit primarily serving Asian American and Pacific Islander communities.

Do you support ethnic-specific organizations?

Yes. We support both pan-Asian/Pacific Islander organizations and those serving specific ethnic communities (Chinese, Filipino, Vietnamese, Korean, Japanese, Indian, Pacific Islander nations, etc.).
