Building digital equity through premium domain gifts and technology access.

Closing the Digital Divide

Technology access remains unequal. While some communities have every advantage, others struggle to establish even basic digital presence. We're here to change that equation.

We Build Piliers gifts premium domain names—assets valued at $500 to $100,000+—to organizations and individuals from underrepresented communities. A professional domain isn't just a web address; it's infrastructure that legitimizes your work and amplifies your voice.

When YourOrganization.org replaces yourorg.freesite.com, funders take notice. Media takes notice. And your community gains a permanent home on the internet.

Communauté Connection

Communities We Serve

We support what we call "pillar communities"—the underrepresented groups that hold up society while often being overlooked:

  • Black communities — Organizations, businesses, and creators
  • Brown and Latinx communities — From immigrant services to cultural organizations
  • Indigenous nations and organizations — Tribal entities and cultural preservation projects
  • LGBTQ+ organizations — Pride organizations, advocacy groups, community centers
  • Women-led organizations — Organisations à But Non Lucratif and social enterprises with women leadership
  • Immigrant communities — Organizations serving newcomers and diaspora groups
  • Disability community — Advocacy, services, and representation organizations
  • Neurodiverse community — Groups supporting and representing neurodivergent individuals

Questions Fréquentes

What free tech resources are available for underrepresented groups?

We Build Piliers offers premium domain names valued at $500 to $100,000+ as gifts to organizations and individuals from underrepresented communities. This includes Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women-led, immigrant, disabled, and neurodiverse communities.

Who qualifies as an underrepresented group?

We support pillar communities including Black, Brown, Indigenous, LGBTQ+, women-led, immigrant, differently abled, and neurodiverse individuals and organizations. Both nonprofits and community projects qualify.

How do I access free tech resources?

Apply through our website by submitting information about your organization or project. Our team reviews applications and matches qualifying applicants with premium domains from our portfolio. The application is free and takes about 10 minutes.
