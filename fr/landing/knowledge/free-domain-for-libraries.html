Free Domain for Libraries

Libraries can receive free premium domains worth $500-$100,000+.

Supporting Library Digital Presence

Libraries are community anchors providing access to knowledge, resources, and connection. A professional domain helps library projects reach readers, share catalogs, and build community around literacy and learning.

Communauté libraries, especially those serving underrepresented populations, often operate with minimal budgets. A premium domain provides the professional foundation for building an effective digital presence.

We Build Piliers supports library projects serving pillar communities, helping build the digital infrastructure that expands access to knowledge.

Questions Fréquentes

Can libraries get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers supports community libraries and library projects, especially those serving underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What types of libraries qualify?

We support community libraries, little free libraries, mobile libraries, prison libraries, specialized collections, digital library projects, and library advocacy organizations. Public libraries with adequate funding may be directed to other resources.

Do you support little free library projects?

Yes. Little free libraries and community-based reading initiatives serving underrepresented neighborhoods are exactly the kind of projects we love to support.
