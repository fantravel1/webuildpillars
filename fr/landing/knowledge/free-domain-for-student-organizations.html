Questions Fréquentes

Can student organizations get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers supports student organizations, especially those led by or serving students from underrepresented communities. We gift premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+.

What types of student organizations qualify?

We support cultural student organizations, identity-based groups, advocacy organizations, service clubs, academic societies, and student publications. Priority goes to groups serving underrepresented student populations.

What happens when student leaders graduate?

The domain belongs to your organization. Establish clear succession procedures, but the domain can serve your student org for years to come.
