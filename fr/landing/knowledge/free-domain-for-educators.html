Free Domain for Educators

Premium domains for those building knowledge in underrepresented communities.

Empowering Educational Innovation

Education is the foundation of community empowerment. Educators from underrepresented communities are doing critical work—often without the resources their peers take for granted.

A premium domain gives your educational project permanence and credibility. Whether you're sharing lesson plans, creating online courses, or building community education programs, YourProgram.edu or YourProject.org signals professionalism to students, parents, and funders.

We Build Piliers gifts premium domains—valued at $500 to $100,000+—to educators from pillar communities. Build your digital classroom on a foundation that lasts.

Questions Fréquentes

Can educators get free premium domains?

Yes. We Build Piliers gifts premium domains valued at $500 to $100,000+ to educators from underrepresented communities. Whether you're building educational resources, online courses, or community education programs, a professional domain establishes credibility.

What types of educational projects qualify?

We support individual educators, educational nonprofits, community education programs, online course creators, tutoring organizations, educational research projects, and knowledge-sharing initiatives led by or serving pillar communities.

Do I need to be a teacher to qualify?

No. We support all knowledge builders—teachers, professors, trainers, curriculum developers, educational content creators, and anyone building educational resources for underrepresented communities.
