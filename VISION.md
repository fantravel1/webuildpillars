## The Application: Vision Over Credentials

Traditional grants favor those with grant-writing experience, institutional backing, and existing resources. We Build Pillars inverts this:

### What We Ask

1. **Who are you?**
   - Your connection to pillar communities (from, for, or both)
   - Your current work or creative practice
   - No 501(c)(3) required â€” we accept individuals, collectives, and informal groups

2. **What will you build?**
   - The domain you want (from our portfolio or request we acquire)
   - Your content vision â€” what "massive beautiful thing" will fill this space?
   - Why this domain specifically serves your mission

3. **How committed are you?**
   - Your proposed publishing frequency
   - Your plan for the first 90 days
   - How you'll measure success

4. **What support do you need?**
   - Domain only? Domain + AI build credits?
   - Do you want our team to build your initial site?
   - Are you interested in mentorship or community connections?

### What We Don't Ask

- Proof of nonprofit status (we verify mission, not paperwork)
- Financial statements (we're gifting, not investing)
- Professional references (your work speaks for itself)
- Prior web development experience (that's what AI credits are for)
