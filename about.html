About We Build Pillars

Building digital equity for the communities that build our world.

Our Story

We Build Pillars is the social impact arm of Pillar Media & Entertainment, born from a simple observation: the organizations and individuals doing the most important work in our communities often have the least professional digital presence.

Over years of building our premium domain portfolio—now valued at over $126 million—we realized we had something unique. Not just assets, but opportunities. Opportunities to level a playing field that has long favored those with resources over those with purpose.

When a grassroots nonprofit uses a free subdomain while well-funded organizations command premium .org addresses, there's an immediate perception gap. When an independent artist can't afford their own name as a domain while corporate entities snap them up, there's a creativity tax being paid by the wrong people.

We're here to change that.

What We Believe

Digital Identity Is Foundational

In today's world, your domain name is often your first impression. It shouldn't be determined by your budget, but by your mission and impact.

Pillar Communities Deserve More

Black, Brown, Queer, Indigenous, neurodiverse, female, migrant, and differently abled creators are the pillars of our society. They deserve digital infrastructure that matches their contributions.

Premium Shouldn't Mean Privileged

A premium domain shouldn't be a luxury. For mission-driven organizations, it's a necessity. We're making that necessity accessible.

Impact Over Optics

We measure success not by domains distributed, but by impact enabled. Every domain we gift should translate to real-world change.

Why "Pillar" Communities?

We chose the word "pillar" deliberately. Pillars don't just support—they're foundational. They're the structure upon which everything else is built. Yet too often, the communities we serve are described in deficit terms: underserved, marginalized, disadvantaged.

We reject that framing.

Black creators have shaped every major cultural movement of the past century. Indigenous knowledge keepers hold wisdom that could save our planet. Queer artists have pioneered aesthetic revolutions. Neurodiverse thinkers see solutions invisible to others. Immigrant entrepreneurs build economies. Disabled innovators create accessibility that benefits everyone.

These aren't communities that need saving. They're communities that need equal access to the tools they've been systematically denied.

A premium domain is one such tool.

Our Approach

We don't just hand out domain names. We build partnerships.

Every domain gift comes with the support needed to make it meaningful. Depending on your tier, that might mean DNS setup guidance, email configuration, hosting recommendations, or full strategic partnership including brand development and technical mentorship.

We believe in meeting organizations where they are. A small community group just starting out needs different support than an established nonprofit looking to professionalize their digital presence. Our four commitment tiers—Foundation, Growth, Impact, and Legacy—reflect that reality.

Pillar Media & Entertainment

We Build Pillars is the philanthropic expression of Pillar Media & Entertainment's core mission. For years, we've been building a portfolio of premium domains—strategic digital assets that represent significant value.

Rather than sit on these assets or sell them to the highest bidder, we've chosen to deploy them where they'll create the most impact: in the hands of mission-driven organizations and individuals from pillar communities.

This isn't charity. It's strategy. When pillar communities thrive, we all thrive. When their digital presence matches their real-world impact, the entire ecosystem benefits.
